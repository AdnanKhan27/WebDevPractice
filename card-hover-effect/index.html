Mewto

A powerful Psychic-type pokemon that was cloned from Mew. It has a savage personality and can Mega Evolve into two different forms.

Rayquaza

A Dragon/Flying-type pokemon that lives in the ozone layer. It can destroy meteors with its Hyper Beam and can Mega Evolve without a Mega Stone.

Lucario

A Fighting/Steel-type pokemon that has high attack and special attack stats. It can sense and manipulate aura, and can Mega Evolve with a Lucarionite.
